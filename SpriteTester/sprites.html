SPRITE TESTER

This is for testing to make sure your sprite is in the correct format. All sprites should have a descripitive name without spaces (use underscore). Below are sprites named draco_malfoy and monarch_butterfly. Once you name your sprite, use it to name your sprite directory. Then put your sprite directory into the sprites/test directory. You'll then need to list your sprite name inside the provided sprites.json file, which loads sprites from the listed directories. Look at the format of these two sprites and their corresponding files to see how your work should be properly configured.
